About us

Kutaisis qvis qarxana is one of the largest importer and exporter of natural stone facing materials in Georgia. Our goal is to build on our history of best quality product and service delivery for our customers, to be the Project Partner – from receiving customized orders of natural stones until the final delivery.

In our factory quarries we excavate natural stones and turn it into unique design facing materials according to your idea and your project. 18 quarries provide a wide variety of products and guarantee environment-friendly natural stone selection...

Our team has been excavating, transforming and selling natural stone products from proprietary or exclusive quarries for more than 10 years...
